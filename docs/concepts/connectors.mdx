# Connectors

A **connector** is a managed ingress: an external source that automatically drops documents into a Sifter [folder](/concepts/folders), where every linked [sift](/concepts/sifts) then runs on them. No cron, no watcher script, no `curl` loop.

<Info>
Connectors are a **Sifter Cloud** feature. Self-hosted installs use the [REST API](/integrations/rest-api) or [Python SDK](/integrations/python-sdk) upload path instead.
</Info>

## Why connectors

The `POST /api/documents` endpoint is fine for one-off uploads, but most real workflows have an *existing* place where documents land — a shared Drive folder, an accounting inbox, a vendor portal that emails PDFs. Connectors wire Sifter directly into those flows so extraction happens as soon as the document arrives.

## Available connectors

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Google Drive" icon="folder-open">
    Watch a Drive folder (shared drive, My Drive, or regular folder). Sifter polls every ~15 minutes and picks up new or modified documents. Read-only scope — Sifter never writes to your Drive.

    [Setup →](/integrations/google-drive)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mail-to-upload" icon="envelope">
    Every Sifter folder gets a unique forwarding address (`folder-abc@in.sifter.run`). Forward any email with an attachment — invoices, receipts, contracts — and the attachments become documents in that folder.

    Available on **Starter+**.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Lifecycle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect">
    In the Sifter web app, open **Connectors** in the sidebar. Pick a provider and authorize — Drive uses OAuth, mail-to-upload generates an address instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure destination">
    Each connection needs a **destination folder** on the Sifter side. Every document the connector pulls in is dropped into that folder, and every sift linked to the folder runs on it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync">
    Drive syncs on a background schedule (~every 15 minutes) and can be triggered on demand. Mail-to-upload fires as soon as an email hits the forwarding address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Revoke">
    Click **Revoke** on the connection card. OAuth tokens are deleted immediately and future syncs stop. Documents already ingested stay in your folder.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What gets ingested

- **Supported formats**: PDF, Word (DOCX/DOC), Excel (XLSX/XLS), PowerPoint (PPTX/PPT), and images (PNG, JPG, TIFF, WEBP). Native Google Docs / Sheets / Slides are skipped — export them to a supported format first.
- Documents longer than 10 pages are counted as `skipped` and reported in the sync result.
- Each successfully ingested document counts against your monthly document quota.

## Connectors vs. direct upload

| You want… | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| A Drive folder to auto-flow into Sifter | **Drive connector** |
| Vendors or teammates to email documents in | **Mail-to-upload** |
| Programmatic control from an existing pipeline | [REST API](/integrations/rest-api) / [SDK](/integrations/python-sdk) |
| One-off manual upload | Web UI drag-and-drop |
